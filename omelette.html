Omelette

Omelettes are an excellent low carb meal to start your day. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • oil
  • veggies (optional)
  • cheese

Steps

  1. pan on high heat with liive oil and butter
  2. add tomatoes
  3. combine 3 eggs in measuring jug
  4. add to hot pan with tomatoes
  5. cook until cooked
  6. add cheese
